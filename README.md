# 🍽️ uBite  
### A calm and personalized ARFID food system

uBite is a compassionate and highly personalized **food tracking and planning application** designed specifically for individuals with:

🧠 **ARFID (Avoidant Restrictive Food Intake Disorder)**  
🧩 **Autism**  
⚡ **ADHD**

Unlike traditional food apps that focus on **calories and nutrition**, uBite focuses on:

✨ comfort  
✨ predictability  
✨ gentle exposure  
✨ reducing food-related stress

The app uses a **calm dark interface with soft blue accents** to create a safe and low-stimulus environment for managing food choices.

All data is stored locally for **maximum privacy and persistence**.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of uBite is simple:

Turn the overwhelming experience of daily eating into something **manageable, predictable, and empowering**.

uBite helps users:

🌱 **Encourage Gentle Exposure**  
Gamified challenges allow users to try new foods or textures slowly and safely.

🧍 **Support Body Autonomy**  
Instead of strict nutritional rules, the app focuses on **how food feels**.

🧠 **Reduce Decision Fatigue**  
When energy is low, the app helps answer the hardest question:

> *“What can I eat right now?”*

---

## 🚀 Main Features

### 📱 Intuitive Tab Navigation

A minimal **bottom navigation bar** lets users switch between the four main areas of the app:

- 🏠 **Home** – Daily dashboard and quick actions  
- 🥑 **Foods** – Personal safe-food database  
- 📅 **Plan** – Weekly meal planning  
- 🎯 **Challenges** – Gentle exposure goals

---

### 🏠 Home — Daily Snapshot

Your **daily overview** designed for quick decisions.

Features include:

- ✨ **Today at a glance**  
  See what meals are planned.

- ⚡ **Quick add meal**  
  Log meals quickly with name, time, and notes.

- 💡 **Suggest something to eat**  
  The app can generate food suggestions based on:
  - time of day
  - preferences
  - available foods
  - energy level

---

### 🥑 Foods — Personal Safe Food Database

The **core of the system**.

Instead of generic food tracking, this creates a **personal food landscape**.

When adding foods you can log:

- 🍽 **Food name**
- 🧠 **How the food feels**
  - Safe
  - Mostly Safe
  - Neutral
  - Can Eat if Needed
  - Want to Try
  - Scared to Try
  - Never Tried
  - Dislike
  - Can’t Eat

- 👃 **Sensory notes**
  - texture
  - smell
  - brand
  - portion size
  - personal reactions

- 🏷 **Dietary tags**
  - Dairy-free
  - Vegan
  - High protein
  - Grain-based
  - Takeout

- 📦 **Stock count**  
  Track what you currently have at home.

#### 🔎 Smart Filtering

The food list can be filtered by:

- ✅ Safe foods  
- 🌱 Vegan  
- 🥛 Dairy-free  
- 💪 High protein  
- 📦 Only foods currently in stock  

You can also **search instantly**.

#### 🍔 Takeout Support

uBite recognizes something important:

> **Eating something is always better than eating nothing.**

Takeout foods can be tracked and suggested as valid options.

---

### 📅 Plan — Weekly Predictability Calendar

Planning reduces anxiety around food.

Instead of overwhelming weekly planners, uBite uses a **single-day view**.

Each day includes slots for:

- 🍳 Breakfast  
- 🥪 Lunch  
- 🍝 Dinner  
- 🍎 Snack  
- 🥤 Snack / Drink  

Users can:

- ✔ tap a slot to add a food  
- ✔ paste recipes  
- ✔ add preparation notes  
- ✔ define portions or temperature (hot / cold)

---

### 🎯 Challenges — Gentle Exposure System

This section supports **slow food exploration**.

Users can create challenges like:

- 🍏 Try a new apple variety  
- 🍜 Eat at a new restaurant  
- 🥗 Try one bite of a new texture  

Each challenge becomes a **level**.

Progress indicators:

- 🟡 *In progress — small steps count*  
- 🟢 *Completed — +1 XP*  

Gamification helps create **positive reinforcement without pressure**.

---

### 💡 Smart Suggestions

The system can generate **food suggestions** based on:

- 🕒 Time of day  
- 🥣 Meal type  
- 📦 Available foods  
- ⭐ Safe food levels  

Example prompt:

> **“What can I eat right now?”**

The system suggests **safe or low-effort options first**.

## 🌙 Calm & Accessible Design

The UI is intentionally designed to reduce sensory overload.

Features include:

- 🌑 **Dark theme**  
  Black and dark grey backgrounds reduce visual stress.

- 🔵 **Soft blue accents**  
  Used for buttons and interactive elements.

- 📐 **Clean layout**  
  Large spacing and simple structure support users with:
  - ARFID
  - autism
  - ADHD

## 🔒 Data Privacy

All user data is stored locally using:

💾 **browser local storage**

This means:

- ✔ no accounts required  
- ✔ data persists after refresh  
- ✔ full privacy  

The system is **future-ready** for backend integration.

Possible backend options:

- 🐍 Python / Flask  
- 🐘 PHP / Laravel  
- 🗄 MySQL database  

This would enable:

- multi-device sync  
- cloud backup  
- account systems
